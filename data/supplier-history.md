## Contract Compliance Notes

- Nordic Provisions has twice attempted to include open-ended price escalation language in renewal addenda (Q2 2022 and Q4 2023). Both instances required negotiation back to capped escalation. ZavaShop procurement policy CP-14 prohibits open-ended escalation clauses.
- EXW Incoterms were proposed by Nordic Provisions in the Q4 2023 contract renewal discussion. ZavaShop Supply Chain rejected and renegotiated to DAP Netherlands DC. Supplier accepted after two-week delay.
- Notice period in current contract is 30 days. ZavaShop standard minimum is 60 days. This was accepted as an exception in 2021 at category manager level. Not yet corrected in current contract.
