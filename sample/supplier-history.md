# ZavaShop Supplier Performance History

Internal procurement reference. Performance data covers the trailing 12 months (June 2025 - May 2026) unless stated otherwise. Ratings: A (Preferred), B (Conditional), C (Watchlist), D (Restricted).

## Harborview Foods & Provisions Ltd

- **Supplier ID:** SUP-00418
- **Categories Supplied:** Ambient Grocery, Edible Oils, Snacks, Beverages, Confectionery
- **Relationship Since:** March 2022
- **Overall Rating:** B (Conditional)

### Delivery Performance

- **On-time, in-full (OTIF) rate:** 82% over the trailing 12 months, against ZavaShop's 95% supplier target.
- Two late deliveries in Q1 2026 breached the agreed lead-time window and triggered service credits under the contract's delivery-penalty clause.
- Short-shipments recorded on three occasions, all on snack lines, later reconciled.

### Quality Incidents

- **August 2024:** A batch of Cold-Pressed Sunflower Oil 1L (ZS-OIL-2207) was flagged for under-fill, averaging 1.5% below the declared 1L volume. Stock was quarantined and a corrective-action plan was accepted and closed in October 2024. No recurrence since.
- One minor labelling discrepancy on Organic Rolled Oats (ZS-GRN-1042) in early 2025 - certification logo placement - resolved without recall.

### Contract Compliance

- One invoice dispute in 2025 over a disputed freight surcharge; resolved in ZavaShop's favour after 6 weeks.
- Harborview has historically pushed for Net 30 terms against ZavaShop's preferred Net 45 for ambient grocery lines.
- Renewal discussions in 2024 stalled because the supplier declined to commit to a capped price-escalation clause.

### Commercial Notes

- Competitive on snack and beverage lines; consistently 3-6% above benchmark on edible oils.
- Financially stable per latest credit check (Dun & Bradstreet score: low risk).
